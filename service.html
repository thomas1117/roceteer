BE THE BEST VERSION OF YOURSELF MORE OFTEN

We love working with entrepreneurial people who want to improve themselves, remove obstacles, achieve their goals and so have a greater impact as a leader in their teams and communities.

MINDSET

Everything starts with your mindset, what are you telling yourself, what are your beliefs? Are you hindering your own ability to flourish and be the best that you can be to truly self-actualize and leverage all your strengths? As trained coaches and NLP practitioners we love asking the right questions to get to the heart of the matter and support your growth.

ACTIONS

We love supporting you to close that knowing-doing gap, where you intellectually know what to do and yet for some reason you do not act, you procrastinate. We support you to leverage your strengths and abilities so that you can be a better leader and inspire action in others.

IMPACT

If you have the right mindset and also start to act appropriately you will create amazing impact for yourself and those around you. We work with you to ensure that this happens and also that you celebrate it so that the actions are reinforced and it becomes a virtuous circle. You achieve your goals, you are happy and this inspires a better mindset and more action leading to yet more results.

MAXIMIZE THE IMPACT OF YOUR COMMUNITY

The thing we love the most is working with entrepreneurial communities whether they be an investment portfolio, progressive community or supporting intrapreneurs inside corporates. At the community level we support the fabric of the community as well as the teams and individuals.

Investment Portfolio

What if your investment portfolio had a passionate team of people that could coach, mentor, train and consult to your teams. A team that could connect all the people inside your investment ecosystem to leverage the power of connection. We love working with investment ecosystems to support the ecosystem to maximize the return on investment for the fund by maximising the return on community within it.

Community

Sometimes the community is not an investment portfolio and instead there is a desire to improve a certain geographic community. We support communities by mapping the community and connecting people as well as running events for the members of the community and coaching people and teams within the community.

Corporate Innovation

Inside any large corporate you will find amazingly talented and creative people that want to be entrepreneurial but can't seem to find the platform to get internal attention. We work with corporates to identify entrepreneurial talent within the company and also create internal incubator programs to support the identification and realization of creativity and innovation from within.
